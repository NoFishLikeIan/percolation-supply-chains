\section[Limits of Shift]{Limits of $G$}

Other limits of $G$ are

\begin{equation}
    \begin{split}
        \lim_{\rho \rightarrow 1} G(\mu, \rho, s) = (\mu, 0) \\
        \lim_{\mu \rightarrow 0} G(\mu, \rho, s) = (0, 0) \\
        \lim_{\mu \rightarrow 1} G(\mu, \rho, s) = (1, 1)
    \end{split}
\end{equation}
